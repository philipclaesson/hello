Klimato


What is it?

Klimato is a project where we are creating an easier way of 1. measuring the CO2e-emissions that are caused by a certain dish and 2. making the measure understandable for anyone. We target restaurants and kitchens in Sweden.

What is my role?

- Development of the concept at large (together with 3 other founders)
- Building klimato.se from scratch.
- Leading the development of the product.
- Creating a graphic profile (except logo)
- Creating all graphic content
